Traceability and Compliance

The traceability  within Abel ERP allows administrators to to verify the history, location, or application of an item by means of documented recorded identification. Abel has the capability to keep track of who does what and when they do it. This allows for significance assurance to an organization and provides a robust structure to be Sarbanes Oxley compliant.
