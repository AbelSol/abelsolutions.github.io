Use the BI Tool of Your Choice

Abel ERP has the flexibility and capability to integrate to your organizations preferred BI Tool. Regardless of what reporting tool your organization would like to utilize Abel Solutions will capture and provide data so that you can see your business information in the ay that is most appropriate for your business.

Be it as simple as spreadsheets, dashboards or as complex as integrated big data sets Abel ERP has the functionality and power to provide the visibility and power to effectively analyze your businesses data.
