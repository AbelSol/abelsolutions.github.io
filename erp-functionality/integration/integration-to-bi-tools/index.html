Abel ERP allows your organizations data to be displayed and analyzed in the most appropriate way. Abel utilizes SQL extraction and easy to use data dictionary to provide simple and repeatable data extractions that can seamlessly integrate with your organizations preferred BI Tools. Abel ERP doesn't restrict you to specific tools and setting up the integration is simple and cost effective. The maintenance of data tables and managing the extractions is also very straight forward.
