Visibility of Stock

Abel ERP gives you genuine visibility across your business Inventory is fully integrated with all other relevant ABEL modules, including General Ledger, Accounts Receivable, Sales and Purchase Orders, Manufacturing, Job Costing, Procurement, Accounts Payable, Web ecommerce and Mobile CRM. You can drill down to view details by date or demand history; totals by item or location; or view balances for multiple periods meaning that relevant information can be sourced swiftly meaning better decisions and improved operational performance with happier customers
