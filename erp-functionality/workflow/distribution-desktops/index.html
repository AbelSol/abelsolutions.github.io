The web based Abel Distribution Desktop enables you to see the current status of your critical business information in one glance, such as the current inventory and all outstanding sales orders and purchase orders for immediate tracking.  The Abel desktop can also show the status and date of every packing slip in the system, and also the individual and collective amount paid on incidentals such as freight and duty.
