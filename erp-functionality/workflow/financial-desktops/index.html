The web based Abel Financial Desktop provides visibility of key financial information in real time, such as all financial totals, revenue  to forecast, gross margin on sales, customer and supplier ageing reports and balances. Having ‘up to the minute’ information on the desktop, without waiting till month-end, provides all the pivotal information in one view, allowing more informed business decisions.

From this desktop you can also save time on financial reporting. You can simply extract reports directly from within the financial desktop, providing immediate and current reports from which to gauge the status of your business.
