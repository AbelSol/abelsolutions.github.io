Abel PLM for Apparel

Abel PLM for Apparel software for fashion, consumer goods and footwear companies spans the entire product development and sourcing process-from concept to customer.

Abel PLM is a fully integrated solution that deliver comprehensive functionality in an intuitive and easy-to-use suite. Everyone who deals with product development or delivery- including product development, sourcing, line planners, marketing, merchandising, sales and executive teams- works with a "single version of the truth" about the product.
