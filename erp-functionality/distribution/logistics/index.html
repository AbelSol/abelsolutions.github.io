Logistical efficiency

Abel ERP for Freight and Logistics helps companies boost their agility, speed and competitiveness. To beat competition and cater to a global marketplace, the freight and logistics industry needs to change continuously. Abel ERP  helps clients achieve faster and sustainable transformation with a focus on achieving outcomes that impact operations at an organizational level—instead of driving value in siloed business segments. By harnessing the power of our in-depth knowledge, people, assets and methodologies, we are able to help companies boost their performance—from issues to outcomes.

Abel ERPs logistics capabilities provide powerful forecasting functionality supporting your long-range planning and helping you to optimize your logistic and warehouse management. With our ERP verticals for logistics you are able to schedule purchases and turnover based on articles and intervals by using an unlimited number of planning models to simulate different future scenarios. Using serial and batch numbers, you can always track article movements within Abel ERP by having so direct access to information on where articles have been used and where they currently are.

Transparent inventory management and optimized logistics:

  • Logistic process and stock improvements through a direct coordination of customer needs and article availability
  • Optimized inventory management by improved transparency through an intelligent article tracking
  • Better material requirements planning by flexible options for managing assortments
  • Simple creation of assortments with a graphical assortment designer
