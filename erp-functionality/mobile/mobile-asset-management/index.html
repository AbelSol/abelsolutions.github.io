Improve Asset Management in Diverse Locations

Abel Mobile solutions allows your organization to monitor and maintains things of value wherever they may be located. Buildings plant and equipment can be monitored and checked against with maintenance histories created and maintenance schedules maintained with the system. Abel ERP allows your organization to systematically operate, maintain, upgrade, and dispose of assets cost-effectively. Abel ERP also allows organizations to manage assets to achieve the greatest return (particularly useful for productive assets such as plant and equipment), and the process of monitoring and maintaining facilities systems, with the objective of providing the best possible service to users.
