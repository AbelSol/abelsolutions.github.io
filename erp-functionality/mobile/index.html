Abel Mobility Solutions

Abel enables you to mobilize sales information direct to and from mobile devices. Abel enables easy access to the vital information needed to deal with customers whenever and wherever required.  Abel allows users to get complete on or offline capability even when not within network coverage. It is simple to implement because it is a complete, flexible application with its functionality and user interface controlled by configurable options. The functionality is simple to deploy over diverse geographic regions.

Abel mobile solutions are very straight forward to  to use. It is intuitive and works in a very similar way to numerous other mobile applications. Because it is easy and intuitive it gets used and improves sales behaviors really quickly. The App allows sales and account information to be updated through mobile devices simply and quickly, removing the traditional barriers of inputting data at another time which invariably never gets done.
