Campaign Building

Abel CRM can help you grow your business by developing more accurately targeted sales and marketing campaigns based on analysis of existing sales data, business categories, customer interests and geographical locations. By analyzing customer purchasing patterns you can generate organic sales and can more effectively build campaigns to up-sell or cross-sell using filters such as:

  • Products purchased or not purchased (within a date range).
  • Product categories purchased or not purchased (within a date range). Customer and Contact Categories
  • Countries, States, Cities, Postcodes
  • Account managers
